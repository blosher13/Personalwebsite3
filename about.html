Personal Details

Here's some more info obout me.

Hi, my name is Blosher Brar. I am 29 years old (as of May 2021) and I live in beautiful British Columbia. I have created this website as way to learn more about web development/programming as well as exhibit my personal projects. Prior to my deep dive into data science, I worked in a number of different industries, including Sales Advisor at Tesla, and Infantry Officer in the Canadian Armed Forces. I have always been passionate about tech and doing a job where I am challanged mentally almost everyday.

In 2018 I began my journey into the tech field and committed to learning everything I could about data science, data engineering, and the programming. I moved from Vancouver to Montreal to begin my studies in the Masters of Data Science program at Université de Montréal's business school (HEC Montreal). Having come from a Biology & Chemistry major in my bachelors, and having very limited programming experience, it was a challenging time to say the least. But it was also a period of high growth, excitment, and trying to learn as much as I could.

While balancing my full-time studies with my part-time job and self-paced trianing, my first year was a crash course in time management. But I managed to stay afloat, and by the end of my second semester, I landed an internship at a Groundswell Cloud Solutions as a Salesforce Consultant. This was a first taste at a real tech company and it was an amzing experience where I gained knowledge on such things as Agile Methodology, software development lifecyle, and version control.

As an intern at Groundswell, one of my tasks was to develop reports and dashboards to help managers track employee time utilization. Using my background in data visualization, and data science, I was able to create dynamic reports and dashboards that reduced the time it took to calculate employee productivity from 16 hours to a few seconds using automated data pipelines that required no extra work by employees. This also provided managers and executives with the abiliy to track department-level productivity as well as track historical trends at the individual, team, and company-wide level.

In Feburary 2021, I began working full-time as a Data Engineer for WithYouWithMe, a consulting company based in Sydney, Australia. I absolutly love this role and feel excited to wake up every day and go to work (for now, that is downstairs in my home office). I have been learning something new almost everyday and it feels great to work on solving tough and challenging tasks. I have mostly been working on Microsoft and Azure based data services. My next goal is to master the art of data architecture as well as learn about other major cloud based services like AWS and Google Cloud.

Any Questions?

Why did you make this website?
I created and deployed this website on a Netlify server so that I could learn more about web app design and back-end development.

In the future I will use this website as a nesting ground for my personal projects, as well as connecting with others that are passionate about tech or data science.

How long have you been practicing data engineering?

I discovered data engineering in 2018 while undertaking my MSc in Data Science, and I have been obsessed ever since.

Over the years I have been developing my knowledge through reading, watching, and creating data engineering projects.

Do you have any experience working with databases?

I have hosted and deployed my own MySQL database with this website. I have a advanced knowledge of SQL queries, and have had experience working with Azure and Power BI

By renting servers from Azure, I have been able to develop my own back end production environment. I believe that data scientists who can own every step of the ML lifecycle will be invaluable in the coming years..

What kind of work do you see yourself doing?

I am interested in building high performance data solutions that can integrate with existing production systems and provide visibility into a company's business lines.

I would describe my perfect job title to be a mix of data engineering, machine learning engineering, and product management. I love finding clever solutions to tough problems, and building a product with an empahsis on business value.
